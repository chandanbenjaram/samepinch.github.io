The Rules

Post/Comment will be removed that...

■is unlawful, libelous, defamatory, sexually explicit, obscene, abusive, violates other's privacy rights.

■is vulgar or inappropriate language.

■promotes dangerous or misuse of services/products.

■degrades others on basis of gender, race, class, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sexual preference or disability.

■is a personal attack or designed to incite others to violate rules

■is disruptive or off topic.

■copyright protected materials which lack ownership.

■link baiting (embedding a link in your post to draw traffic to your own site)

■spamming in nature

If you break the rules we may contact you and ask you to fix the problem immediately via email. If you don't, your account may be suspended or deleted, your username forfeited, and your IP address may be permanently banned from logging into SamePinch. We reserve the right to enforce or not to enforce these rules in whatever way we see fit, at our sole discretion. Also, SamePinch's rules and terms&conditions do not create a duty or contractual obligation for us to act in any particular manner. And we reserve the right to change these rules at any time. So please play nice, be respectful, and have fun.

If you notice abusive behavior, please report it immediately by emailing abuse@samepinch.co and we will look into it, and take necessary action as soon as possible.
